How Verification Works

1
Read the full story and examine the listed evidence files before voting.
2
Vote to verify if the reporting appears factual, sourced, and credible.
3
Dispute a story if you have specific reason to believe it is inaccurate or fabricated.
4
Your votes contribute to the public Trust Score displayed on every article.
5
Stories reaching 70%+ verification are marked Verified. Below 40% triggers editorial re-review.
